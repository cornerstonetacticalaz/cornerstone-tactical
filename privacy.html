Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2026

Cornerstone Tactical ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide through our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or contact our business.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us, request services, or submit information through our website. This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Information submitted through contact forms
  • Information necessary to complete firearm-related services

We may also automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of website visits

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through our website may be used to:

  • Respond to customer inquiries
  • Provide requested services
  • Process firearm transfers and related transactions
  • Maintain required ATF records
  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations

3. Firearms Transaction Information

Information collected during firearm transactions is handled in accordance with applicable federal laws and ATF recordkeeping requirements.

Customer information obtained during firearm transfers is used only for lawful business purposes and required compliance obligations.

4. Information Sharing

Cornerstone Tactical does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information may only be shared when necessary to:

  • Comply with federal, state, or local laws
  • Respond to lawful government requests
  • Complete required firearm transfer procedures
  • Work with service providers necessary to operate our website

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services to improve functionality, process forms, analyze website traffic, or provide notifications.

These services may include, but are not limited to:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Form processing services
  • Analytics services

Third-party services maintain their own privacy policies governing the collection and use of information.

6. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve user experience and understand website traffic patterns.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some website functionality may be affected.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect personal information submitted through our website. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. Cornerstone Tactical is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of third-party websites.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes become effective when posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact Cornerstone Tactical through the contact information provided on our website.
