About Cornerstone Tactical

Professional • Honest • Consistent

Cornerstone Tactical LLC is a veteran-owned Federal Firearms License (FFL) business proudly serving Buckeye, Arizona, and the surrounding communities. We provide secure, compliant firearm transfer services with an emphasis on professionalism, integrity, and outstanding customer service.

Every transfer is handled in accordance with all federal, state, and local laws. Whether you're purchasing from an online retailer, arranging a private-party transfer, or need assistance with firearms services, our goal is to make the process simple, secure, and stress-free.

Veteran Owned

Built on integrity, accountability, professionalism, and service to our community.

FFL 07 Manufacturer

An FFL Type 07 allows us to manufacture firearms, perform gunsmithing services, customize firearms, assemble complete firearms, and conduct retail firearm transfers while remaining fully compliant with federal regulations.

Appointment Only

Operating by appointment allows every customer to receive dedicated, one-on-one service in a safe and professional environment.

Firearm Transfer Process

Simple, secure, and compliant every step of the way.

1

Purchase Your Firearm

Buy from your preferred online retailer or another licensed dealer.

2

Select Cornerstone Tactical

Have the seller ship your firearm to Cornerstone Tactical LLC.

3

Schedule an Appointment

Once your firearm arrives, we'll contact you to arrange pickup.

4

Background Check

Complete ATF Form 4473 and all required federal and Arizona requirements.

5

Take Your Firearm Home

After approval, you'll leave with your firearm knowing everything was completed legally and securely.
